Channels are good for coordination, but not always for speed.

Under high load:
* many goroutines use the same channel
* sends/receives block
* goroutines wait
* scheduler overhead grows
* latency increases

Common problem:
* one shared channel on a hot path

Important:
* channels are usually slower than mutexes/atomics for simple shared state

> Channels scale poorly when too many goroutines contend on the same channel.
