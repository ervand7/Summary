> I usually start with a modular monolith: one Django project, split into domain-oriented
apps with clear boundaries.

How I structure it:

* Organize apps by business domain
* Keep views thin, move logic to service layer
* Share only common infrastructure, not business logic
* Extract microservices only when needed (scale, ownership, deployment)

One-liner:

> I use a modular monolith with domain-based apps and evolve to microservices only when it
provides real value.
