Health checks are periodic probes a load balancer uses to decide which instances
should receive traffic.

How they work:
* LB calls endpoints like `/health`
* Healthy instances receive traffic
* Unhealthy ones are removed

Key insight:
* Health checks must be lightweight and reliable
* Bad health checks can worsen outages by stressing dependencies

Interview version:
Health checks let load balancers route traffic only to healthy instances, but they
must stay simple and dependency-light to avoid amplifying failures.
