ORM:
* Faster CRUD development (less boilerplate)
* Reduces simple SQL mistakes
* Consistent data access style across team
* Convenient for basic queries and relations
* Not ideal for complex or high-load queries

Raw SQL:
* Full control
* Maximum performance
* transparency (you see exactly what runs)
* Better for complex queries and tuning


👉 Better rule of thumb:

> ORM for simple CRUD and productivity,
> SQL for complex and performance-critical parts.
