👉 A consumer can exist outside a consumer group.

Typically used for:
* debugging
* admin utilities
* batch / one-off jobs

Comparison:
| Consumer group     | Without group |
|--------------------|---------------|
| Auto rebalance     | ❌            |
| Offset storage     | ❌            |
| Scalability        | ❌            |
| Production default | ✅            |
| Debug / batch      | ✅            |

### Interview phrasing
> Kafka consumers can operate either as part of a consumer group or independently with
manual partition assignment; only consumer groups provide automatic rebalancing
and scalable consumption.

In production, consumer groups are almost always used.
