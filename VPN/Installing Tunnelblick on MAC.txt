Установка Tunnelblick на MAC https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P-WaRNM9PJs

1) download Stable from https://tunnelblick.net/downloads.html
2) while open DMG select double click on Tunnelblick
3) click 'i have conf files'
4) look the icon appearance on top panel of the desktop
5) go here https://freevpn4you.net/ru/
6) select Швеция
7) select TCP
8) drag the downloaded file onto the icon Tunnelblick on top panel of the desktop
9) select все пользователи
10) click Tunnelblickicon and then click соединить
11) paste login and password which are on the same place where you selected TCP here https://freevpn4you.net/ru/locations/sweden.php
