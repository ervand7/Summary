Circuit Breaker = “fuse” for an external service

👉 Imagine:

* You call a service (e.g., payments)
* It starts failing / slowing down

---

### Without circuit breaker

* You keep sending requests
* Everything slows down more
* Your system also starts failing ❌

---

### With circuit breaker

* Detect too many errors
* Stop calling the service for a while
* Return error or fallback immediately ✅

---

### States (simple)

* Closed → normal
* Open → stop calls
* Half-open → try a few requests

---

### One-liner:

👉 *“If a service is failing — stop hitting it, let it recover”*
