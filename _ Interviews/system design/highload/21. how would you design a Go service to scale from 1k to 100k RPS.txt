Main ideas:
* stateless service
* bounded goroutines
* minimal blocking I/O
* aggressive caching
* async processing for heavy work

Important:
* avoid global locks
* use connection pool limits
* apply backpressure
* use load balancing and horizontal scaling

Watch:
* p95/p99 latency
* error rate
* GC
* pool exhaustion

> High RPS comes from controlled concurrency and low latency, not from unlimited goroutines.
