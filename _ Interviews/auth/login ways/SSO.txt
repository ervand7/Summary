SSO (Single Sign-On) = log in once, get access to multiple apps

---

### 👉 Simple example

You log in to Google →
then you can open Gmail, YouTube, Drive without logging in again

---

### 👉 Key idea

* One login → many services
* A central system handles authentication (IdP)
