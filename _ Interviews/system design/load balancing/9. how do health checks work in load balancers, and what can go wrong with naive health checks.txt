### How health checks work
* LB sends regular requests to a health endpoint (e.g. `/health`)
* Instance replies healthy / unhealthy
* Unhealthy instances are removed from routing
* Healthy ones receive traffic again

```
LB ‚Üí /health ‚Üí Service
```
