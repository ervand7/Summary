Kafka Controller. Is just one of the brokers (is chosen automatically by Kafka).
- One per cluster
- Manages cluster state
- Elects partition leaders
- Handles broker failures


Consumer Group Coordinator (is chosen automatically by Kafka)
- One per consumer group
- Manages group state
- Triggers rebalancing
- Assigns partitions
- Stores offsets


Key difference:
- Controller → cluster & partitions
- Coordinator → consumers & rebalancing

One-liner:
> Controller manages the Kafka cluster; Coordinator manages consumer groups.
