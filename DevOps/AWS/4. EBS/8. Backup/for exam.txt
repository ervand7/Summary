 - Consolidation. Use AWS Backup to back up AWS services, such as EC2, EBS, EFS,
 Amazon FSx for Lustre, Amazon FSx for Windows File Server, and AWS Storage Gateway.

 - Organization. You can use AWS Organization in conjunction with AWS Backup to
 back up your different AWS services across multiple AWS accounts.

 - Benefits. Backup gives you centralized control, letting you automate your
 backup and define lifecycle policies for your data. You get better compliance,
 as you can enforce your backup policies, ensure your backup are encrypted, and
 audit them once complete
