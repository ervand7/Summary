What it does:

* Allows short bursts
* Enforces long-term average rate

* Burst-friendly
* Industry standard (Envoy, NGINX)

Cons:
* Slightly more complex logic

Typical use:
* Public APIs, gateways, per-user limits

* Bursts are not tolerated

* Protecting sensitive downstream systems
