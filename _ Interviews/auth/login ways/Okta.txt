Okta = a service that manages login (authentication) for your apps

---

### 👉 Simple idea

Instead of building login yourself, you use Okta:

* user logs in via Okta
* Okta tells your backend:

  > “this user is authenticated”

---

### 👉 What it does

* handles SSO
* supports SAML / OAuth / OpenID Connect
* manages users, passwords, MFA

---

### 👉 One-liner

👉 Okta = external login system you trust to authenticate users
