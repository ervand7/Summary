1Password = password manager

---

### 👉 Simple idea

It stores all your passwords securely so you don’t need to remember them

* you remember one master password
* it autofills logins for sites/apps

---

### 👉 What it does

* stores passwords, API keys, secrets
* generates strong passwords
* syncs across devices

---

### 👉 One-liner

👉 1Password = app that safely stores and autofills all your passwords
