* Debit — money is taken from an account
* Credit — money is added to an account
* Balance — total amount on the account
* Available balance — spendable amount (after holds)
* Hold / Authorization — temporary lock on funds (not yet charged)
* Capture — final charge after authorization
* Refund — money returned after a charge
* Reversal — cancel before the transaction is completed
* Chargeback — forced refund via bank (customer dispute)
* Transaction — any operation (debit/credit)
* Settlement — final clearing between parties
* Ledger — record of all transactions
* Double-entry — every transaction has two sides (debit/credit)
