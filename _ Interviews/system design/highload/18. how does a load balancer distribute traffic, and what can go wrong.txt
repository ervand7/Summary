A load balancer spreads requests across multiple servers.

Common strategies:
* round-robin
* least connections
* hashing / sticky sessions
* weighted routing

Can be:
* Nginx / HAProxy
* cloud LB
* Kubernetes Service

Important:
* health checks
* retries
* timeouts

Problems under load:
* uneven traffic
* retry storms
* bad health checks
* LB itself becomes overloaded

> A load balancer spreads traffic and helps handle failures.
