* Digital signatures (authenticity/integrity):
  ✔️ Sign with private key
  ✔️ Verify with public key

* Encryption (confidentiality):
  ✔️ Encrypt with public key
  ✔️ Decrypt with private key

👉 So:

* Sign → private → verify → public
* Encrypt → public → decrypt → private

That’s the full correct picture.
